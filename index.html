About Me

Hi, I'm Jarne, a 17-year-old guy passionate about web development. I'm from Belgium and currently learning the ropes of creating awesome websites. I have a particular interest in front-end development and enjoy crafting engaging user interfaces with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Besides web development, I also have some experience in Python programming. I've tinkered with Python to build small projects and automate tasks. I find it fascinating how coding empowers us to bring ideas to life and solve problems in creative ways.

I'm excited to continue my journey in the world of web development and explore new technologies and frameworks along the way. Feel free to connect with me if you share similar interests or have any questions. Let's learn and grow together!

Future Goals

In terms of my future goals, I am incredibly passionate about web development and have a clear vision of where I want to go. One of my primary objectives is to create a personal project that holds great value to me – a blog app/website where individuals can share their thoughts, experiences, and ideas through engaging blog posts. I believe in the power of storytelling and how it can connect people from all walks of life. By developing this platform, I aim to foster a vibrant online community where individuals can express themselves and engage in meaningful discussions.

Additionally, I have set my sights on working at a big tech company, where I can leverage my web development skills and collaborate with like-minded professionals. Being part of a renowned tech company will provide me with valuable opportunities to learn, grow, and contribute to innovative projects that have a significant impact on the digital landscape. I am eager to immerse myself in a dynamic work environment, surrounded by talented individuals who share my passion for pushing the boundaries of technology.

Projects

Login Signup Form

I created a responsive login form and signup form that provide a seamless user experience. The forms incorporate modern design principles and validation techniques to ensure data integrity. You can check out the project live at Login and Signup Form.

Topsy Turvy Game

As a fan of game development, I ventured into creating a game using Pygame called 'Topsy Turvy.' Although it is not yet published, you can explore the project's source code on GitHub. Topsy Turvy offers an engaging gaming experience with unique gameplay mechanics.

Google Clone (Design)

I undertook the challenge of recreating the design of Google's search engine. Although it does not have the full functionality, the clone demonstrates my attention to detail and ability to replicate complex interfaces. You can view the project live at Google Clone and explore the source code on GitHub.
